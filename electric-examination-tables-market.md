<p><strong>The Electric Examination Tables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Table</li><li>3-Table</li><li>4-Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric examination tables come in various configurations tailored for medical settings, primarily categorized as 2-table, 3-table, and 4-table designs. The 2-table model typically features basic height adjustment for general examinations. The 3-table design adds flexibility, often allowing for tilt and trendelenburg positioning for more specialized procedures. In contrast, the 4-table variant offers the most advanced features, including additional adjustments for various angles and positions, catering to diverse medical disciplines, enhancing patient comfort, and improving clinician accessibility during examinations.</p></p>
