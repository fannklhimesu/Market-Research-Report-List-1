<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Double Layer Capacitor</li><li>Hybrid Capacitor</li><li>Pseudo Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-capacitor market for electric buses comprises three main types: electrochemical double layer capacitors (EDLC), hybrid capacitors, and pseudo-capacitors. EDLCs store energy through the electrostatic separation of charges, offering high power density and rapid charging capabilities. Hybrid capacitors combine features of EDLCs and batteries, providing improved energy density while maintaining quick discharge rates. Pseudo-capacitors utilize faradaic reactions for energy storage, offering higher energy density than traditional EDLCs, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient energy management in electric buses.</p></p>
