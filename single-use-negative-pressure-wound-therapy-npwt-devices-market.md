<p><p>Single-use Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) devices feature two main market types: canister-free and with canister. Canister-free devices utilize a built-in mechanism to collect exudate, eliminating the need for separate canisters, which enhances convenience and reduces waste. In contrast, NPWT devices with canisters utilize external containers to collect fluid, allowing for larger volumes of exudate to be managed. Both types facilitate wound healing by applying negative pressure, promoting blood flow, and reducing infection risks.</p></p>
