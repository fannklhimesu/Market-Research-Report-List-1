<p><p>The Anterior Thoracolumbar Stabilization System market encompasses Monoaxial and Polyaxial systems, which serve to stabilize and support the thoracolumbar spine during surgical procedures. Monoaxial systems feature fixed axes that allow for limited motion, providing robust support for spinal alignment. In contrast, Polyaxial systems offer multi-directional adjustability, enabling surgeons to achieve optimal placement and positioning of implants while accommodating various anatomical variations. Both systems are essential for enhancing stabilization outcomes in spinal surgeries.</p></p>
