<p><p>The GaAs solar cell epitaxial wafer market encompasses three main types: single junction, double junction, and triple junction. Single junction cells utilize one layer of material, offering simplicity and lower costs but limited efficiency. Double junction cells combine two layers to enhance performance, achieving better conversion rates. Triple junction cells incorporate three layers, maximizing efficiency by harnessing a broader spectrum of sunlight, making them suitable for space applications and high-performance systems. Each type serves specific applications, balancing cost and efficiency.</p></p>
