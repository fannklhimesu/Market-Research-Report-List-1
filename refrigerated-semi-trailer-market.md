<p><p>The refrigerated semi-trailer market primarily comprises two types: single temperature and multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers. Single temperature refrigerated semi-trailers maintain a consistent temperature throughout the entire trailer, ideal for transporting goods that require uniform cooling, such as frozen foods. In contrast, multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers are equipped with multiple compartments, allowing for the transport of various products at different temperatures simultaneously. This versatility is particularly beneficial for logistics companies handling diverse inventories, enhancing operational efficiency in cold chain management.</p></p>
